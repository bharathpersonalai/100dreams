Sindhura Sanjeevani

Sindhura Sanjeevani stands as a lifeline for those in urgent need, bringing hope and healing through timely blood donations and health support.

Every drop collected is a promise , a promise to save lives, ease pain, and give families another chance to hold their loved ones close. With over 250 lives touched, our mission continues to beat with compassion, care, and an unwavering commitment to humanity.

Organ Donation

Raising awareness and facilitating the life-saving process of organ donation.

Every organ donated is not just a part of the body ❤️ it is a heartbeat restored, a breath regained, a future rewritten. Through our mission, we strive to ensure that no precious life is lost waiting for a second chance to live.

Dinabandu

Dinabandu extends a compassionate hand to grieving families, easing the burden during one of life’s most difficult moments. By providing financial assistance between ₹10,000 and ₹25,000 for last rites, we ensure that every soul is bid farewell with dignity, respect, and love.

About Our Founder

Meet the visionary behind 100 Dreams

Krishna Maninei

Visionary & Change Maker

Krishna Manineni is not only a celebrated hero in the film industry but also a true hero in real life. With a deep sense of compassion and a strong belief in giving back to society, he founded 100 Dreams seven years ago with a mission to serve people from all walks of life.

Under his leadership, the organisation has introduced several impactful initiatives:

  • Annashooktham – Providing hot, nutritious meals to those in need, with over 70,000 meals donated so far.
  • Sindhura Sanjeevani – Organising blood donation camps where 250+ donors have contributed to saving countless lives.
  • Punarapi – Spreading awareness and facilitating organ donation, inspiring many to pledge for a noble cause.
  • Deenabandhuvu – Extending support for the dignified final rites of the underprivileged.

Beyond these initiatives, Krishna has personally contributed towards medical aid, supported individuals in financial distress, and even donated to places of worship, including temples and churches, strengthening community bonds.

Balancing his career in cinema with his passion for service, Krishna Manineni continues to prove that true success is measured not just by fame, but by the lives one touches. His vision is to inspire more people to join hands in building a compassionate and selfless society.
